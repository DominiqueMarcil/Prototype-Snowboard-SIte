Could you imagine life without font variables...

Font variables, the new up and coming cornerstone to modern webdesign.
These font variables have brought about a significant transformation in how we
perceive and engage with web typography. Font variables have opened up a world of creative
possibilities, while also enhancing website readability, accessibility, performance, flexibility, and
responsiveness. Not to mention what a time and space saver font variables have become.
